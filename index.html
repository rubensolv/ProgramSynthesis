SynProS - Synthesis of Programmatic Strategies

Welcome to the 1st Synthesis of Programmatic Strategies Competition.

SynProS is one track in the MicroRTS AI Competition. This track has been created to motivate research questions in the synthesis of strategies (scripts) using techniques of program synthesis in a minimalist RTS environment, called MicroRTS. We propose a domain-specific language for specifying programmatic-guided synthesis (SynProS) problem, introduced bellow:

In the MicroRTS repository, there is a complete framework that implements the DSL to be used as part of the synthesizer that will be created by each competitor. The repository's wiki contains examples and guidance about how to use the MicroRTS and the framework for this competition.

Competition's Workflow
  • The synthesizer will run for 24 hours in an specific map to synthesis a programmatic strategy.
  • When all synthesizers finished the synthesis phase, all the programmatic strategies will be splited by maps.
  • The programmatic strategies are evaluated combining two scores: A score in a Round-Robin tournament against the other programmatic strategies in the specific map and the score obtained in the interpletable evaluation.
  • The winner of the competition is the synthesizer able to produce, in general, the best solution for each map.
